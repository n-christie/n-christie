# Nick Christie
**Researcher · Lund University | Ageing & Health → Corporate Finance**

I use quantitative methods to study how people, firms, and institutions make decisions — and what gets in the way. Currently at Lund University working on ageing and housing research, with a corporate finance postdoc starting January 2027.

## 🔬 Research interests
- Housing, relocation, and active ageing (RELOC-AGE project)
- Geographical variation in frailty and home care across Swedish RegSO areas
- Corporate finance: profit caps, windfall levies & investment decisions
- Causal inference in observational data (IV, DiD, panel methods)

## 🛠 Tools
R (ggplot2, tmap, tidyverse) · Stata · Git · Swedish register data · Panel econometrics
